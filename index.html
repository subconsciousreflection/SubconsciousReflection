Subconscious Reflection

Implicit bias is the unconscious and automatic predisposition individuals possess towards certain groups, influencing their judgments and actions without conscious awareness.

Further below are valuable resources to learn more about the implicit bias and access to Project Implicit. Through Project Implicit, you are able to take exams that reveal implicit biases by assessing the speed and accuracy of participants' responses to stimuli associated with various concepts such as race, gender and others. The goal is to bring awareness to unconscious biases, fostering self reflection and encouraging conversations about the impact of these biases on individual behavior and decision making.

Project Implicit is an ongoing research initiative that provides a platform for online Implicit Association Tests (IATs), designed to measure individuals' implicit biases across various social and cultural domains.
